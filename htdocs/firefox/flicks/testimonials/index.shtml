Tell the world about Firefox!


  • What to do? Record and submit your own short testimonial telling the world what you love about Firefox. Your testimonial will be added to the Testimonial viewer (which will be available very soon) for others to see by clicking your location on the globe. Just read the simple instructions on how to use the Testimonial tool and fill out the submission form telling us what you’re going to talk about. Your testimonial will be reviewed by Spread Firefox Administrators and, after their review, published for others to see.
  • When to do it? The Testimonial campaign will be open until February 21st 2006 so plan on checking back to see what testimonials others have recorded. We’ll be giving out Firefox gear each week to submitters of the most creative and entertaining testimonials.
  • Why do it? To tell the world about why you use Firefox and to be eligible for great prizes! Between now and the end of the contest we’ll be announcing weekly winners who will receive an Amazon.com gift certificate for $50 as well as Firefox gear from the Mozilla store. The grand prize winner will receive a $500 gift certificate from Amazon.com. Other great prizes will be given out along the way. At the end of the contest we’ll also pick the best of the best, and feature those testimonials on Mozilla web sites.

  • How? Visit the Firefox Flicks Testimonial tool where you’ll be guided through the Web cam set up process. If you don’t have a Web cam you may be able to just plug in your digital video camcorder. If you need to buy a Web cam, try Amazon.com for a wide selection of Web cams under $100.
