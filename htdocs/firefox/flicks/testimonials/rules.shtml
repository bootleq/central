Entries must be submitted online by 11:59 p.m. Pacific Time on February 21, 2006. The authorized account holder is the natural person who is assigned to the email address by an Internet access provider, online service provider, or other organization that is responsible for assigning email addresses or the domain associated with the submitted email address.


5. JUDGING AND SELECTION OF WINNER

All entries will be judged by a panel of Spread Firefox Administrators. Entries will be judged on the following four criteria: 1) Originality (50%); 2) Production Quality (10%); 3) Script (20%); and 4) Delivery (20%). One winner will be selected at the conclusion of each week of the Campaign. One Grand prize winner will be selected at the conclusion of the campaign, on or about February 28, 2006. In the event of a tie, finalists will be re-judged based on creativity of Entry. The decisions of the judging panel are final on all matters relating to the Campaign.

6. PRIZES

Weekly Prizes :
At the conclusion of each week, a weekly winner will be awarded a $50 Amazon.com gift certificate. There are 10 weekly winner prizes to be awarded by the conclusion of the campaign.

 

Grand Prize :

The sponsor will award the grand prize winner a $500 gift certificate to Amazon.com. In addition, the winning videos may, at the Sponsor’s sole discretion, be used in the worldwide Firefox 1.5 marketing campaign.
