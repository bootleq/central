What is the last date I can submit my video testimonial?
We will be gathering testimonials for as long as people are interested. To be considered for prizes, entries must be submitted by February 21, 2006.

How do I record my video testimonial?
To enter a testimonial, simply visit http://video2.spreadfirefox.com and follow the instructions.

How can I view my video testimonial?
We'll be publishing the video testimonials soon.  Sign up here and we'll let you know.  You can also check back here for details. 

How many times can I enter a video testimonial?
We’re accepting only one entry per person or group.

Is the video testimonial campaign open to International entries?
Yes. Please see the Terms and Conditions section for more information.

Will the video testimonials be judged?
Yes, the testimonials will be judged based on originality, production quality, script, and delivery.

 Who are the judges?
 The judges are Spread Firefox Administrators.

Will there be prizes?
Yes, we will be awarding prizes each week that are judged best in each of the criteria around originality, production quality, script and delivery. At the conclusion of the campaign we will be selecting a grand prize winner.

What kind of prizes can I win?
 Weekly prizes will include Firefox gear and an Amazon.com gift certificate. The grand prize will include a $500 gift certificate to Amazon.com.

How will I be notified if I win?
Winners will be notified by email.

For how long will my video testimonial be available to be viewed?
We intend to keep the testimonial site going for up to six months. After that, we may make the videos available in other formats at Spread Firefox. We may also use the best testimonials in our worldwide Firefox 1.5 marketing campaign.
